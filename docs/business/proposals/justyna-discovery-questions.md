# Justyna — open discovery questions

These are questions the Monday call did **not** pin down. The proposal
quotes a fixed price *assuming* a sensible default for each one. If any
answer comes back different, NexFortis re-quotes within two business
days. The list is also useful as a kickoff agenda once she accepts.

## A — Wix account

1. Which **Wix Premium plan** is your site on right now? (I need
   **Core, Business, or Business Elite** for the store to work —
   "Light" doesn't allow selling.)
2. Is the plan in **your name** or someone else's? I'll need you to
   either add me as a contributor or share screen during setup; I do
   not need your password.
3. Is your **domain registered through Wix**, or is it pointed at
   Wix from another registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap)?

## B — Products

4. How many **products** are you launching with on day one? (The
   Standard package covers up to 15.)
5. Do you have **photos for every product** ready to go? What
   format / dimensions? Any background removal needed?
6. Do products come in **variants** (sizes, colours, packs)? If yes,
   roughly how many variants per product?
7. Do you track **inventory counts**, or is everything made-to-order
   / unlimited stock?
8. Are any products **digital** (downloads, gift cards) vs. physical?

## C — Payments

9. Do you want to use **Wix Payments** as the primary card processor?
   (It's the smoothest path. Stripe / Square / Moneris are possible
   but cost extra to wire in.)
10. Do you have a **business bank account** in your business name to
    receive payouts? (Wix Payments needs one for KYC.)
11. Do you want a **second** payment option — PayPal, manual
    e-transfer instructions on the order page, or both?

## D — Shipping & fulfilment

12. Where do you ship — **Ontario only / Canada-wide / US / further**?
    The base price covers Canada-wide flat or by-weight; US and
    international are add-ons.
13. Do you want **flat rate** ($X for any order), **by weight**, or
    **calculated rates** via a courier API (Canada Post, etc.)?
    Calculated rates are an upgrade.
14. What's your **handling time** between order and shipment? (One
    business day? Three?)
15. Do you offer **local pickup** in Etobicoke as a free option?

## E — Tax

16. Are you registered for **HST in Ontario**? Do you have your
    HST number? (If yes, Wix can collect HST automatically; if no,
    we leave tax off until you register.)
17. Do you sell to other provinces with different tax rates? (Wix
    can handle the table; I just need a yes/no.)

## F — Brand & content

18. Do you have a **logo file** (SVG / PNG with transparent
    background)? If not, I'll use the existing site's image.
19. What **brand colours and fonts** does your existing site use?
    I'll match them.
20. Do you have **store policies** drafted (shipping, returns,
    privacy)? If not, I'll start from a template and you'll edit.
21. Any **About the Shop** copy you want on the new Shop page intro?

## G — Operations after launch

22. How will you **receive order notifications** — phone, email, or
    both? Same address as the Wix account?
23. Who else needs **access to fulfil orders** (staff, family, a
    bookkeeper)?
24. Do you want me to **ship a test order to myself** on launch day
    using a real card, then refund it? (Recommended — it proves
    end-to-end the whole stack works.)

## H — Marketing (out of scope, for awareness)

25. Are you running any **ads** today (Meta, Google, Kijiji boost)?
    If yes, where do they currently land — your existing Wix site,
    a Facebook page, somewhere else?
26. Do you collect an **email list** today? (If yes, I'll make sure
    new orders flow into it via Wix's built-in tools.)
27. Anything else you mentioned on the call I should re-confirm in
    writing before kickoff?

---

### How to use this list

- **Before sending the proposal:** skip — the proposal already states
  the assumed defaults, so don't ambush her with a 27-question form.
- **After she accepts:** send this as the kickoff agenda. It also
  doubles as a checklist for the kickoff call so nothing is missed.
- **If she asks "what do you need from me?" before accepting:** point
  her at sections **A, B, and F** only — the other sections can wait
  until kickoff.
