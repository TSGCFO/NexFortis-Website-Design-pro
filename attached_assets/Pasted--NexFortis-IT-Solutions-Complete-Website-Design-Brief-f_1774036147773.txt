
# NexFortis IT Solutions — Complete Website Design Brief for Replit Agent

## Company Overview
Design a professional, modern, and conversion-optimized business website for **NexFortis** (17756968 Canada Inc.), a Canadian IT solutions company headquartered at 204 Hill Farm Rd, Nobleton, ON L7B 0A1, Canada. NexFortis provides end-to-end technology services for small, medium, and enterprise-level businesses. The website must position NexFortis as a trusted, one-stop technology partner that simplifies IT for businesses of all sizes.

---

## Brand & Design Direction

- **Brand Name:** NexFortis
- **Tagline Suggestion:** "Your Business. Our Technology. Limitless Growth." (or generate a better one)
- **Color Palette:** Professional and modern — use a primary dark navy/deep blue (#1a2744 or similar), accented with a vibrant teal/electric blue (#00b4d8 or similar) and clean white (#ffffff). Secondary accent of a warm gold or orange for CTAs.
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Poppins, or Manrope for headings; Open Sans or Nunito for body text).
- **Design Style:** Sleek, corporate-modern with generous white space, smooth scroll animations, subtle gradient backgrounds, and micro-interactions on hover. Think Stripe/Vercel-level polish. Use professional SVG icons (Lucide, Heroicons, or similar) throughout.
- **Responsiveness:** Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile. Mobile-first layout principles.

## Technical Requirements

- **Framework:** Use React (Next.js or Vite) or a modern framework suitable for Replit deployment
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS for utility-first styling
- **Animations:** Framer Motion for smooth page transitions, scroll-triggered animations, and hover effects
- **Icons:** Lucide React or Heroicons
- **Forms:** Functional contact form (can use Formspree, EmailJS, or similar for email delivery)
- **SEO:** Proper meta tags, Open Graph tags, structured data (JSON-LD), semantic HTML, sitemap.xml, robots.txt
- **Performance:** Lazy loading for images, code splitting, optimized bundle size
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant — proper ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, color contrast
- **Deployment:** Ready for Replit deployment with proper build configuration
